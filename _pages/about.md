I am a PhD candidate in Engineering Sciences at the Pontificia Universidad Católica de Chile (PUC), currently conducting research as an intern at the AIDOS Lab at the University of Fribourg. 

As an Affiliated PhD Researcher at Chile's National Center for Artificial Intelligence (CENIA). My work focuses on the intersection of Deep Learning, and Topological Data Analysis (TDA). I am particularly interested in analyzing diverse Neural Network phenomena through topological lenses.
